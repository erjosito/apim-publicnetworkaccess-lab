VAL-MODIFY - Proving the Policy 'modify' effect is impossible for this alias
Date: 2026-07-02

CMD: az policy definition create --name apim-modify-pna-test --mode Indexed --rules @modrules.json
  (rules use effect=modify, operation addOrReplace on field
   'Microsoft.ApiManagement/service/publicNetworkAccess' = 'Disabled')

OUT ERROR (InvalidPolicyRuleModifyDetails):
  The policy definition 'apim-modify-pna-test' has operations referring to aliases that
  are not modifiable: 'Microsoft.ApiManagement/service/publicNetworkAccess'.

CONCLUSION: Azure Policy REJECTS the modify definition at CREATION time - you cannot even
save it. This is the definitive proof (beyond the alias metadata in S0) that a modify
effect cannot be used to set publicNetworkAccess on APIM. This is why DINE is required.
